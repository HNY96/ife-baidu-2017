Game Rule:

Press the 'Start' and enter commands into the input box to control the player.

We have the rules:

  • GO: make the player go straight on
  • TUN LEF: make the player rotate counterclockwise 90 degrees
  • TUN RIG: make the player rotate clockwise 90 degrees
  • TUN BAC: make the player rotate clockwise 180 degrees
  • TRA LEF: make the player move to the left box nearby without rotate
  • TRA RIG: make the player move to the right box nearby without rotate
  • TRA TOP: make the player move to the above box nearby without rotate
  • TRA BOT: make the player move to the below box nearby without rotate
  • MOV LEF: make the player face to the west and go straight on
  • MOV RIG: make the player face to the east and go straight on
  • MOV TOP: make the player face to the north and go straight on
  • MOV BOT: make the player face to the south and go straight on
